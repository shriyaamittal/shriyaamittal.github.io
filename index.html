Shriyaa Mittal, PhD

I am a Journal Manager at AIP Publishing working on two open access journals, APL Machine Learning and APL Energy.

Before this, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the laboratory of Jack W. Szostak at Harvard Medical School/Massachusetts General Hospital/HHMI. My research focuses on investigating molecular mechanisms of RNA and their implications towards our understanding of the origins of life.

I received my PhD in Biophysics from the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign working with Prof. Diwakar Shukla studying membrane transporter proteins using computational methods. Prior to this, I got my Bachelors in Computer Science and Masters in Computational Natural Science at IIIT, Hyderabad, India.
